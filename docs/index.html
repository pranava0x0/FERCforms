How to use this explorer ▾
  • Read a report: click a card to expand its thread — each finding of noncompliance, with the staff recommendations nested beneath, quoted verbatim.
  • Spot the trends: the Top patterns strip ranks the issues FERC raises most. Tap one to narrow the stream to reports with that issue.
  • Filter & search: narrow by industry, FERC form, audit type, function, or year — or type a company or keyword in Search. Picks within one filter widen the results; picks across different filters narrow them.
  • Trace the source: every card links to FERC's original PDF via View on eLibrary; Copy citation grabs a reference.
